About the Kindness APP

In todays world with all the meanness going on it is hard to have faith in humanity. We read stories on news websites on the internet about all kinds of crimes being committed all over the world along with people getting ripped off by someone else. At times we may even witness these things ourselves. This app is designed to help restore faith in humanity!!!

How to Use the Kindness APP

This app is very simple to use. If you witness an act of kindness just simply open the app whenever or wherever you are. Give the app permissions to your location, select the type of kindness you witnessed: a gift, service, time given to another person, a simple touch, or a kind word. Click add, and your GPS location will be recorded in the map with the type of kindness you selected.
